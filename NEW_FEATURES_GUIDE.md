## 🆕 What's New?

### 1. 🎥 Enhanced Video Learning
**Location**: Career Hub → Any Career → Videos Tab

**What's New**:
- Every video now has a "💡 Why watch this:" description
- Understand the value before watching
- All videos are verified and relevant to the career path

**How to Use**:
1. Go to Career Hub
2. Select a career (Software Engineer, Data Analyst, etc.)
3. Click "Videos" tab
4. Read the description to understand what you'll learn
5. Watch the video or open on YouTube

---

### 2. 🤖 AI Mentor - Now with Superpowers!
**Location**: Dashboard → AI Mentor Chat

**New Features**:

#### Upload Files
- Click "Upload" button
- Supported: Images, PDFs, Documents (max 5MB)
- AI will offer options:
  - Generate Notes
  - Create Summary
  - Generate 3D Diagram
  - Create Quiz
  - Analyze Resume (if it's a resume/CV)

#### Special Actions
- **Create Image** 🎨: Generate visual concepts
- **Deep Research** 🔍: Get comprehensive explanations
- **Thinking Mode** 🧠: Step-by-step problem solving

**How to Use**:
1. Go to AI Mentor
2. Click "Upload" to add files OR
3. Click special action buttons for specific modes
4. Type your question or request
5. Get intelligent responses!

**Example Prompts**:
- "Analyze my resume and give me a score"
- "Create notes from this PDF"
- "Generate a quiz on React hooks"
- "Deep research on machine learning algorithms"

### 3. 🏆 Achievement System
**Location**: Dashboard → Achievements

**What It Does**:
- Tracks your learning milestones
- Unlocks badges as you progress
- Shows progress for incomplete achievements

**6 Achievements**:
1. ⚡ **Fast Learner** - Complete 5 challenges in one day
2. 🏆 **Quiz Master** - Score 100% on 3 quizzes
3. 🔥 **Consistent User** - Use app 7 days in a row
4. 🎯 **Code Warrior** - Complete all hackathon challenges
5. ⭐ **Knowledge Seeker** - Read all notes for one career
6. 🏅 **Career Explorer** - Explore all 4 career paths

**How to Use**:
1. Click "Achievements" on dashboard
2. View your unlocked badges
3. Check progress on locked achievements
4. Complete tasks to unlock more!

---

### 4. 📊 Progress Tracker
**Location**: Dashboard → Skill Progress

**What It Shows**:
- **Overall Progress**: Your average completion across all subjects
- **Career Skills**: Progress in 4 career paths
- **Subject Completion**: Detailed breakdown by subject
- **Stats**: Tasks completed, challenges done, streak, study time

**How to Use**:
1. Click "Skill Progress" on dashboard
2. View your overall progress percentage
3. Check individual career skill progress
4. See subject-wise completion
5. Track your stats at the bottom

**Use It To**:
- Identify areas needing more practice
- Celebrate your progress
- Plan your next learning steps
- Stay motivated with visible growth

---

### 5. ⚡ Daily Challenge
**Location**: Dashboard → Daily Challenge

**What It Does**:
- Presents a new challenge every day
- Rotates through coding, design, and concept challenges
- Awards points for completion
- Tracks your daily streak

**Challenge Types**:
- **Coding**: Write code to solve problems
- **Design**: Create user interfaces
- **Concept**: Explain technical concepts

**How to Use**:
1. Click "Daily Challenge" on dashboard
2. Read today's challenge
3. Click "Show Hint" if you need help
4. Write your solution in the code editor
5. Click "Submit Solution"
6. Earn points and maintain your streak!

**Tips**:
- Complete challenges daily to maintain streak
- Use hints wisely
- Take your time to think through problems
- Come back tomorrow for a new challenge

---

### 6. ✨ Smart Suggestions
**Location**: Dashboard → Smart Suggestions

**What It Does**:
- Analyzes your learning activity
- Recommends next steps
- Suggests relevant content
- Prioritizes recommendations

**Suggestion Types**:
- **High Priority**: Critical next steps
- **Medium Priority**: Helpful additions
- **Low Priority**: Optional explorations

**How to Use**:
1. Click "Smart Suggestions" on dashboard
2. Review personalized recommendations
3. Read the reason for each suggestion
4. Click action button to follow suggestion
5. Check back regularly for updated suggestions

**Example Suggestions**:
- "Learn React Hooks" (based on completing React basics)
- "Practice Data Structures" (to maintain momentum)
- "Explore UX Design" (complements your skills)
- "Complete Advanced Notes" (finish what you started)

### Common Questions

**Q: How do I unlock achievements?**
A: Complete the tasks listed in each achievement. Progress is tracked automatically.

**Q: Can I skip daily challenges?**
A: Yes, but you'll break your streak. Try to complete them daily!

**Q: What files can I upload to AI Mentor?**
A: Images (jpg, png, gif), PDFs, and documents (doc, docx, txt) up to 5MB.

**Q: How are smart suggestions generated?**
A: Based on your learning activity, completion rates, and time since last practice.

**Q: Can I see my progress history?**
A: Currently shows current progress. History tracking coming in future updates!
